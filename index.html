Quesadilla

  1. Serves 1
  2. 15 minutes
Ingrediants Quantity Preparation
Tortilla 2
Ched. Chz. 1 slice break into 4 parts
Hot Pepper 1/4 of one
Oregano & Thyme A Pinch of Each . Dried
Oil 1 tbs

Directions

  1. Heat a skillit on medium
  2. add oil
  3. lay 1 tortilla flat
  4. space out pieces of cheddar evenly
  5. Sprinkle Hot pepper, oregano, and Thyme
  6. Lay the second tortilla on top
  7. flip after 5 minutes
  8. remove after 5 minutes
